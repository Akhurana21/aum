Our Process

The AUM 7-Step Packaging Method.

1

Step 1

Technical Requirement Profiling
Weight, size, center of gravity, sensitivity, shipment type, risks.
2

Step 2

Site Dynamics Study
Forklift access, crane lift points, pathway constraints, space assessment.
3

Step 3

Engineering the Structure
Choosing ideal thickness, reinforcement, pallet/box type, moisture solutions.
4

Step 4

Precision Material Selection
Graded wood, tested belts, certified wire ropes, export-safe materials.
5

Step 5

Controlled Manufacturing
Machine-cutting, alignment check, reinforcement fitting, squareness testing.
6

Step 6

On-Site Professional Execution
Lashing, shrink/tank wrapping, boxing, palletizing — executed by trained teams.
7

Step 7

Final Validation & Handover
Quality check, photographs, documentation & dispatch coordination.
This system ensures every job is safe, uniform, predictable and high-quality — regardless of client size.
