Leading Wooden Packaging

Experts Since 1978


AUM Industrial Packaging is an industrial packaging solutions provider headquartered in Thane Vagle Industrial Estate, delivering engineered wooden packaging and cargo protection services across Maharashtra.
Rooted in a longstanding family tradition established in 1978 by Mr. Bajirao L. Mane, we combine decades of practical industry knowledge with a modern, structured and technically driven approach to packaging.
We specialize in solutions that ensure safety, stability and export-readiness for machinery, components and industrial cargo in transit.
Here, our commitment and persistent service in delivering quality packaging solutions which we customized according to the safety of transportation of goods and machines, has empowered us to come up as one of the trusted and reputed manufacturers in our industry sector.

Our quality products with variety, service delivery and their competitive prices have also helped us to meet the expected demands of clients who have retained us more than a decade.


Operational Strengths

AUM Industrial Packaging operates with a flexible, well-organized setup designed to handle industrial packaging requirements smoothly and efficiently.

  • Dedicated workshop for pallets, boxes & crate fabrication
  • Skilled technicians for lashing, wrapping & machinery packing
  • Reliable raw material sourcing ensuring uninterrupted production
  • On-call field team for urgent packaging needs
  • Strong coordination system for fast dispatch & execution
  • Quality-driven workflow ensuring accuracy on every order
Our operations are built to scale as client requirements grow, ensuring consistent performance and reliability.
